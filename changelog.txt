#Version 0.3.8
=== FINAL release ===
Fixing up code and removing half-implemented and deprecated mechanics. CD10 will be re-released under the redesigned core system.

- Removed Encumbrance.
- Removed HitLocation.
- Removed complex checks.
- Implemented simplified trait checks. Left-click on a trait checkmark to enable that trait for the next check. Right click to reverse trait. Perform skill check as usual. 
- Simplified combat tab.
